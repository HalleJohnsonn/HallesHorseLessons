Why should I take lessons?

Riding horses is beneficial to the mind, body, and soul. Riding horses reinforces balance and coordination, improves mental health, reduces stress, and improves mood. And this is just the beginning! Learn the importance of showing compassion and empathy to our fellow equine friends and reap the benefits of being one with nature!
